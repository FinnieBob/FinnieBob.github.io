About Me

Robert Finnie is a highly experienced Senior Software Engineer with over 30 years of professional software development experience. He is a full-stack developer with expertise in a wide range of operating systems, languages, and design methodologies. Robert has a proven track record in innovative design and programming and has also worked as an MSc course designer and lecturer. He is an IET published author on Digital Signal and Image Processing and has competency in multiple programming languages including C, C++, C#, Python, Fortran, Pascal, and BASIC. Robert has experience in Unity 3D, VR, Oculus, and has developed for various platforms such as PC, Android, Linux, and UNIX. He has a proven track record in designing and developing software and hardware solutions for a wide range of applications, primarily in the fields of defence, command and control systems (C2), robotics, war gaming, simulations, and VR. Robert is currently an independent software consultant and developer, and has worked for the Royal School of Signals (UK MoD), NATO C3 Agency and Cranfield University. He was a member of the team that won the "MoD Grand Challenge 2008" and has multiple publications in the IEEE journal. Robert has also supervised MSc students and served as a Viva examiner.
